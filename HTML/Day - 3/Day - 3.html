The Avengers are a team of superheroes from Marvel Comics, first assembled in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) to defeat Loki's Chitauri army. The original MCU lineup included Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye. The team has expanded and evolved over time, facing numerous threats and undergoing internal turmoil, as chronicled in their comics and major films.

Guardians of the Galaxy

This is all about Guardians of the Galaxy
Guardians of the Galaxy Consists of 9 Members
Guardians of the Galaxy is a superhero team from Marvel Comics, best known for a series of films produced by Marvel Studios that follows a ragtag group of intergalactic criminals as they work together to save the galaxy. The core team includes Peter Quill (Star-Lord), Gamora, Drax the Destroyer, Rocket Raccoon, and Groot, though the team's composition changes throughout the series.

TVA

This All About TVA
TVA Stands For Time Variance Authority
The Time Variance Authority (TVA) is a fictional organization from Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) that monitors and polices timelines. Its primary purpose is to preserve the "Sacred Timeline" and prevent the creation of dangerous alternate timelines or variants, which it does by arresting and punishing those who disrupt the timeline. The TVA was created by a being known as "He Who Remains" to prevent multiversal war by a Kang variant.
